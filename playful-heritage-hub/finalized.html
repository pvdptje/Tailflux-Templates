About Heritage Haven

We are a volunteer-driven organization dedicated to collecting, preserving, and sharing the stories that shaped our region. Through exhibitions, publications, and collaborative programs, we bring the past to life for learners of all ages.

  • Founded in 1974
  • 10,000+ archived items
  • 200+ active members
