About the Artist

Meet Elena Rose

With over 8 years of experience specializing in wedding photography, I believe that the most beautiful photos come from authentic moments. My approach is unobtrusive yet attentive, capturing the raw emotions and fleeting glances that make your day uniquely yours.

Based in the heart of the city but available worldwide, I'm passionate about telling love stories through my lens. Every couple has a unique journey, and I'm honored to be the one to preserve those precious memories for generations to come.
