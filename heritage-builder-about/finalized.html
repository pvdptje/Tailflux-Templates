Our Family Story

Founded by John and Mary Peterson in 1975, Built Right Construction began as a small family operation with big dreams. What started with a single truck and a passion for quality work has grown into a respected name in the construction industry.

Today, their son Michael and granddaughter Sarah continue the legacy, bringing three generations of expertise to every project. We believe that building isn't just about structures—it's about building relationships and communities.
