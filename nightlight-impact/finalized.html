Our Journey

From humble beginnings to nationwide impact - our story of growth and transformation

2020

Program Launch

Started with 5 schools and 500 children in underserved communities, laying the foundation for our literacy revolution.

2021

Expansion Phase

Grew to 50 schools and reached 10,000 children across the state, establishing our presence in multiple districts.

2023

National Recognition

Received national award for excellence in childhood literacy programs, validating our innovative approach to education.
