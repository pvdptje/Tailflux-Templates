Essential Travel Tips

💵

Currency

Indonesian Rupiah (IDR). Cash is preferred in local markets.

🌡️

Weather

Tropical climate. Dry season: April-September, Wet season: October-March.

👗

Dress Code

Modest clothing for temples. Cover shoulders and knees.

🚗

Transport

Rent scooters or use ride-hailing apps like Gojek and Grab.
