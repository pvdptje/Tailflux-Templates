Essential Travel Tips

Make the most of your Bali adventure with these practical tips and local insights

Currency

Indonesian Rupiah (IDR). Cash is preferred in local markets. ATMs widely available in tourist areas.

Weather

Tropical climate year-round. Dry season: Apr–Sep, Wet season: Oct–Mar. Pack light clothing.

Dress Code

Modest clothing for temples. Cover shoulders and knees. Sarongs available at temple entrances.

Transport

Rent scooters or use ride-hailing apps like Gojek/Grab. Private drivers are great for day trips.
