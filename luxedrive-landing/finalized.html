Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you hit the road

What documents do I need to rent a car?

You’ll need a valid driver’s license, a government-issued ID, and a credit card in the renter’s name.

Is insurance included in the price?

We offer multiple protection packages during checkout. Basic liability is included where required by law.

Can I modify or cancel my booking?

Yes. You can modify or cancel up to 24 hours before pickup. Flexible rates allow free cancellation.

Do you offer delivery and collection?

We can deliver to hotels and residences in select cities. Add delivery during checkout if available in your area.
