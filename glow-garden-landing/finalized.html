What We Offer

Our garden is more than just plants - it's a thriving community space with something for everyone.

Education Programs

Rooted in Community

Since 2015, Bloomfield Community Garden has been a place to grow healthy food, share knowledge, and celebrate the seasons together.

  • Accessible raised beds and shared tools
  • Organic, pollinator-friendly practices
  • Free beginner classes and mentoring
