Programs We Offer

Toddler Program

Ages 18 months - 3 years

Nurturing environment for our youngest learners to develop independence, social skills, and foundational cognitive abilities through hands-on exploration.

Primary Program

Ages 3 - 6 years

Comprehensive Montessori curriculum focusing on practical life skills, sensorial development, and foundational academic preparation in a prepared environment.

Elementary Program

Ages 6 - 12 years

Advanced studies in cosmic education, mathematics, language arts, and cultural subjects fostering critical thinking and collaborative learning.
